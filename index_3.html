Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

Are materials included in the workshop fee?

Yes — every workshop fee covers all beads, wire, tools, and finishing hardware you need to complete your piece. You only bring your hands (and your creativity).

Can beginners join?

Absolutely. Most of our guests have never beaded before. Sessions are kept small so Saakshi can guide everyone step-by-step, at their own pace.

How long does a workshop last?

Most sessions run between 2 and 2.5 hours, depending on the workshop. The exact duration is listed on each workshop card above.

How do I confirm my booking?

Fill the booking form, complete payment via the UPI QR code, and send your payment screenshot on Instagram or WhatsApp. Your seat is confirmed once we reply back to you.
